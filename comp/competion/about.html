About Us

A Tamil Nadu hackathon is a coding competition or event that is organized in the state of Tamil Nadu, India. The purpose of these hackathons is to encourage innovation, creativity, and problem-solving among students and professionals in the fields of technology and engineering. Some examples of Tamil Nadu hackathons are: GUVI TNCPL: A hackathon for free upskilling in coding, AI, data science, full-stack development, and AutoCAD, in partnership with the Government of Tamil Nadu and Anna University1. Naan Mudhalvan Niral Thiruvizha: A hackathon for engineering students across the state, organized by the Tamil Nadu Skill Development Corporation (TNSDC) and Anna University2.
